Email Template

Description on the build and how our modular system works

 // Email Templates
  Our templates were built entirely modular, with interchangeability being a key component.
  I designed master templates that would be used for every email, and then 
  created dynamic content blocks that would be used to populate the content.
  The master templates include locked portions of the email that would never change, 
  such as the header, footer, and navigation bar.
  The dynamic content blocks would be used to populate the content, 
  such as the hero image, call to action, and body content.
  This allowed for easy scalability and maintainability of our email program.

  // Dynamic Content Types
  We have a total of 300+ dynamic content blocks that are used to populate the content of the emails.
  Each content block is designed to be reusable across multiple emails, and can be 
  easily swapped out to change the look and feel of the email.
  The types of content blocks we have include:
  - Hero Image
  - Hero Image Mobile
  - Hero Image Tablet
  - Hero Image Desktop
  - Hero Image Alt Text
  - Body Content
  - Body Content Mobile
  - Body Content Tablet
  - Body Content Desktop
  - Body Content Alt Text
  - Call to Action
  - Call to Action Mobile
  - Call to Action Tablet
  - Call to Action Desktop
  - Call to Action Alt Text
  - etc.

These are all readily available in package manager, can be exported and 
reimported back into the system to avoid time consuming menu diving through manual duplication.
