Graeme's a horticulturalist with likely many hidden aspirations which is characteristically validated in his recent absence.

It's probably been 8 years since I've seen him.

He told me about the time he spent in Japan. For days he would fish, revisiting the same spot time and time again. He fished every day and caught nothing in a 2 year span. He didn't seem bothered by it. In fact I think he found solace in reconnecting with nature, a quality that clearly hasn't left him since he was a kid, one that was curious in what the world had to offer. It's something that I commend him for, I find that it's tough to find people who have progressed in life naturally, who stay true to themselves.

Had me wondering: if Graeme made his prize catch, would he have released it?

If it came down to it, I'd throw it back in anyways. Just to go through all the motions again. I feel like he probably would've too.
